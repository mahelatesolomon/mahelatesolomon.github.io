Hawaii

Hawaii is one of the Unites States most traveled to destinations. The state brings over 5 million visitors per year according to state government data. The tourist population brings the island much needed revenue, and even makes up a good portion of their economy. The island offers tourists multiple fun excursions through its sunny island. One of it's most famous attractions is the islands beautiful beaches.The tropical paradise is home to many resorts and hotels as it grows in its tourist population. The island is also very well known for its big blue waves that bring surfers from all over the world to compete.
